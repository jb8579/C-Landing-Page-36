Privacy Policy for Powerball.com.au

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, Powerball.com.au.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit Powerball.com.au, we may collect personal information that includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, location data, and browsing behavior. This information helps us to provide you with a more personalized experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To enhance your experience on our website.
  • To send you updates and promotional offers (with your consent).
  • To improve the content and functionality of our website.
  • To comply with legal requirements and protect our rights.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze user behavior, improve user experience, and deliver targeted advertising. By continuing to use our site, you consent to our use of these technologies. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting business, or servicing you. These third parties are obligated to keep your information confidential and secure.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@powerball.com.au.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the last update will be indicated at the top of the policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at support@powerball.com.au.
